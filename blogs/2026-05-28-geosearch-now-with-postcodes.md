Hi everyone, following on from our announcement a few months back of several significant improvements to our geosearch / autosuggest widget , I am pleased to report another major improvement: our geosearch / autosuggest widget now supports searching for postcodes! In some countries postcodes are how a huge portion of end users think about location. Supporting them natively means fewer dropped queries and a smoother experience for everyone.
